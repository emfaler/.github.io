About Me:

With years of experience in a variety of sectors, including the Air Force, civilian aviation, and the service industry, I gained a passion for teamwork and problem solving. During the Spring of 2021 I completed the full stack web developer course at We Can Code It in Cleveland, Ohio. I felt that my background would lend itself well to web development and completing the course would give me skills I needed to thrive in today’s workforce. Throughout course I was challenged to develop foundational skills in a variety of languages as well as use remote technologies to collaborate on class projects. I now look forward to building on my education and bringing those skills into the workplace.
